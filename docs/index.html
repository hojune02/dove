What's inside

Everything you need for
daily spiritual reflection

Daily Scripture Quotes

A curated library of scripture and spiritual quotes. Tap through verses with a gentle fade animation whenever you need a moment of reflection.

Prayer Reminders

Set a daily alarm for your chosen prayer time and repeat days. Local notifications keep your practice consistent without relying on any external server.

Liked Quotes

Heart the verses that speak to you most. Switch on "liked only" mode to cycle through your personal collection of meaningful scripture.

Personal Profile

Tailor the experience to your faith journey. Set your name, faith practice, preferred topics, and personal goals — all stored privately on your device.

Share Inspiration

Pass along any verse to friends and family with a single tap using the native iOS share sheet.

Privacy First

Your preferences never leave your phone. No account required, no backend server. Everything is stored securely on-device using local storage.
